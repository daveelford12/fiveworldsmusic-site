Low Country Soul

Low Country Soul blends modern gospel warmth with gritty blues guitars and late-night soul grooves, creating music that feels both timeless and fiercely alive. Their songs swing between heartbreak and celebration, carried by soaring harmonies and deep southern swagger. Standout tracks like Back to the Start, Lift me Up, and Brand New Mercy highlight their ability to turn raw emotion into something joyful, powerful, and unforgettable.
