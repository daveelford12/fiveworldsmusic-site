The Sailor’s Refrain

Drums like distant thunder, voices like weathered rope — The Sailor’s Refrain builds shanties that feel lived-in, heavy, and deeply human. Their music blends storm-tossed grit with moments of aching beauty, telling tales of loyalty, loss, and the eternal pull of the sea. From the fierce spirit of Mad Peggy Malone to the mournful grandeur of Ballads of the Wide Water, their sound is steeped in salt and sorrow and at times, joyful comaradarie.
